## Introduction

Tink is a service that allows **initiating payments (bank transfers)** and performing secure financial operations, such as **bank verifications and account validations** (including *Account Check* and *Business Account Check*).

## Supported currency

* **EUR (Euro)**

## Available features

### Payments

Consists of **single payments** through bank transfers. This type of operation allows a customer to make a payment from their bank account to a beneficiary, all within a guided and secure flow.

### Account verification (Account Check / Business Account Check)

Account verification through Tink allows confirming that a bank account is valid and that it belongs to the expected type of holder. There are two variants within the same feature:

* **Account Check** (default): to verify individual accounts.
* **Business Account Check**: to verify business accounts.

#### Usage requirements

To use account verification, the following is required:

* Have the **Account Check** feature enabled on the platform account.
* Perform an operation with **amount 0** (`amount = 0`) through the Tink payment flow.

#### How to specify Business Account Check

By default, the verification is interpreted as a **normal Account Check** (individuals). If you want to perform a **Business Account Check** instead of a normal Account Check, you must specify it through the `extra_data.payment.is_business` field in the request, setting it to `true`.

```
"extra_data": {    "payment": {        "is_business": true    }}
```
