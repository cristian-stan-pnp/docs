Welcome to the Paynopain documentation. Follow these steps to integrate our payment gateway into your business, from the first test to the actual collection.

## Create your test account

Before you start, you will need access to our Sandbox environment. You can create your test account in two ways:

- **Self-registration**: Register directly at [portal.paynopain.com/register](https://portal.paynopain.com/register)
- **Support**: Contact our support team to get your test account and API keys

### Get your credentials from the portal

If you already have a Paynopain account, you can obtain your credentials from the Paynopain portal by following this link:

[Paynopain Portal -> Developers](https://portal.paynopain.com/settings/developers)

![Developers section in the portal](/images/es/seccion-desarolladores.png)

## Request your production account

A production account allows you to accept real payments from your customers. Since Paynopain is a regulated financial entity, we must verify your business before activating you.

Before requesting the move to production:
*   Verify that your business model meets our requirements.
*   Make sure you have completed the Sandbox tests satisfactorily.
*   Contact our sales team to start the process.

## Sign the contract and get activated

If your application is approved, we will send you the service contract. Depending on your integration (especially if you process cards directly), you may need to complete a PCI-DSS security questionnaire.

Once we receive the signed contract and the necessary documentation, we will give you access to your **Production Dashboard**.

## Get ready to collect

To start accepting real payments, you must configure your production account. **Important**: Sandbox configurations are not automatically copied to Production.

Use this checklist before launching:
*   [ ] Configure your payment methods and checkout in the production dashboard.
*   [ ] Update the endpoints in your application to point to the production URLs.
*   [ ] Perform a real test transaction (of low amount) to verify the complete flow.

## No-Code Tools

If you prefer not to perform a technical integration, you can use our direct tools:

### Payment Links
Generate secure payment links and send them to your customers via email, SMS, or social networks.
